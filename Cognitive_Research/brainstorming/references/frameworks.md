# Cognitive Frameworks for Brainstorming

当用户需要深度思考时，请参考以下框架进行引导。

## 1. First Principles (第一性原理)
*   **Core Question**: "What is the fundamental truth here?"
*   **Usage**: 剥离所有假设和类比，回归最基础的事实（物理限制、经济动机），从头构建解决方案。适用于颠覆性创新。

## 2. Inversion (反向思维)
*   **Core Question**: "How could we guarantee failure?"
*   **Usage**: 通过设想最坏的情况（如何让系统崩溃、如何让用户流失）来发现系统的脆弱点，从而设计鲁棒的方案。

## 3. MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive)
*   **Core Question**: "Are there any gaps or overlaps?"
*   **Usage**: 在列举功能或场景时，确保分类相互独立且完全穷尽，避免遗漏关键路径。

## 4. SCAMPER (改进现有方案)
*   **Substitute**: Can we replace a component?
*   **Combine**: Can we merge features?
*   **Adapt**: Is there a similar solution elsewhere?
*   **Modify**: Can we change the scale or shape?
*   **Put to another use**: Can this serve a different purpose?
*   **Eliminate**: What can we remove? (YAGNI)
*   **Reverse**: Can we rearrange the workflow?

## 5. Second-Order Thinking (二阶思维)
*   **Core Question**: "And then what?"
*   **Usage**: 不只看直接结果，要推演长期后果和系统性连锁反应。
