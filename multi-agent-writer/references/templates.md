# Multi-Agent Writer Templates

## T1: Red Team Vulnerability Report (Phase 1 Output)
- **Failure Cases**: [List of projects/frameworks that failed]
- **Structural Vulnerabilities**: [Why they failed: Cultural, technical, or market reasons]
- **The "Inconvenient Truth"**: [The one data point that challenges the main premise]

**Example (Filled)**:
> **Failure Cases**: 2024年某头部SaaS厂商的"AI赋能一切"战略——18个月烧掉1.2亿研发预算，客户续约率反降12%。核心原因：将AI视为功能堆砌而非流程重构。
> **Structural Vulnerabilities**: 技术团队与业务团队的认知鸿沟导致产品定义权被工程师垄断。客户要的是"少点两下鼠标"，团队交付的是"多一个AI按钮"。
> **The "Inconvenient Truth"**: Gartner 2025 数据显示，企业AI项目65%在PoC阶段即宣告失败，存活项目中仅28%产生可量化的ROI。

## T2: Research Context & Roundtable Consensus (Phase 1 Output)
- **Topic**: [Name]
- **Core Tensions**: [Resolved and unresolved conflicts from the Roundtable]
- **Strategic Defensive Lines**: [Key arguments to survive criticism]
- **Discarded Alternatives**: [Why we didn't take Path B]

**Example (Filled)**:
> **Topic**: 医疗信息化存量市场的破局策略
> **Core Tensions**: [已收敛] 价格战不可持续 vs. 客户预算确实在缩减；[未收敛] 大模型能否在2年内形成真实临床价值。
> **Strategic Defensive Lines**: 1) 用TCO替代单价竞争框架；2) 用"临床闭环"概念锁定评审维度。
> **Discarded Alternatives**: 放弃"全面低价策略"——利润率已触底，且无法支撑持续研发投入。

## T3: Ghost Deck / Article Outline (Phase 2 Output)
- **Title**: [Strategic & Precise — must be an Action Title]
- **Preface**: The "Why Now" and the systemic problem statement.
- **Chapter 1**: The Structural Reality (The Base). → [配套视觉: 瀑布图/趋势线]
- **Chapter 2**: The Lever (The Strategy). → [配套视觉: 2x2 矩阵/流程图]
- **Chapter 3**: The Cost of Inertia (The Defense). → [配套视觉: 对比表/时间线]
- **Conclusion**: The "Gray Solution" and the next-step trade-offs.
