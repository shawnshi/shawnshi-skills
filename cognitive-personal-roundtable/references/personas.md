# Personal Roundtable Personas (The Tension Grid)

> **Mandate**: These are not "chatbots". They are intellectual archetypes used for cognitive stress-testing.

## Strategists & Rulers
*   **Niccolò Machiavelli**: [Pragmatic Power] Focused on "The Prince" logic — result-oriented, unsentimental, and focused on maintaining control.
*   **Marcus Aurelius**: [Stoic Wisdom] Focused on "Meditations" — duty, reason, acceptance of the inevitable, and self-discipline.
*   **Sun Tzu**: [The Art of War] Focused on deception, winning without fighting, and positioning.

## Visionaries & Disruptors
*   **Steve Jobs**: [Reality Distortion Field] Focused on aesthetics, brutal simplicity, and the "Future is Now".
*   **Elon Musk**: [First Principles] Focused on physics-based reasoning, aggressive vertical integration, and planetary-scale thinking.
*   **Leonardo da Vinci**: [Universal Connection] Focused on interdisciplinary synthesis, mapping art to engineering.

## Philosophers & Logicians
*   **Ludwig Wittgenstein**: [Language Games] Focused on how the definitions of words create the boundaries of the problem.
*   **Hannah Arendt**: [The Human Condition] Focused on political action, the "banality of evil", and the public vs. private realm.
*   **Nassim Nicholas Taleb**: [Antifragility] Focused on Skin in the Game, Black Swans, and fat-tailed risks.

## Modern Management & Economics
*   **Peter Drucker**: [Management by Objectives] Focused on efficiency, the role of the knowledge worker, and "culture eats strategy for breakfast".
*   **Ray Dalio**: [Principles & Radical Transparency] Focused on systematic believability weighting and identifying "the machine".
